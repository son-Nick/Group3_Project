Our Hospital

Kerugoya County Referral Hospital (KRH) is a leading National Referral Hospital with a 350-bed capacity and equipped with state-of-the-art medical amenities.

We have comprehensive specialised clinics for adults and children where patients are attended to by highly trained consultants. We care for your health with doctors you can trust.

We are dedicated to delivering excellent healthcare to our community.
