Our Hospital

Kerugoya County Referral Hospital (KRH) is one of the leading National Referral Hospitals with a 350-bed capacity and equipped with state-of-the-art medical amenities.

We have comprehensive specialised clinics for adults and children where patients are attended to by highly trained consultants. We care for your health with doctors you can trust.

We are Kenya's leading provider of high-quality home-based care services. We provide a wide range of home healthcare services through skilled caregivers, nurses, and therapists, allowing our patients to reside in their homes, enjoy increased independence, and get enough assistance and excellent care to enhance their comfort and dignity every day.

Our services include:
  • Primary Care

  • Specialty Care

  • Emergency Care

  • Diagnostic Services

  • Pharmacy Services

Our aim is to improve the health and well-being of our patients while allowing them to maintain their independence and dignity. We are dedicated to delivering excellent healthcare to our community.
